The Craft of Tea

An Exploration of Tea Blending

by Hunter Larco — founded on September 2, 2014

Tea is an ancient, aromatic beverage formed from the steeped leaves and buds picked throughout the world.

Nowadays tea is becoming ever more popular thanks to the commercial brand Teavana. However, as tea becomes more popular, more food additives, artificial sweeteners, and artificial flavoring are finding their ways into commercial tea ingredients.

As an avid tea enthusiast and DIY junkie, my response will be a journey into the art of tea blending.

In my excitement towards blending in my own kitchen, I searched online for resources to start with. Shocked, I found none! Any books focused either on the history of tea or it's medicinal purposes. The only book somewhat suitable: The Art of Blending by Blend Master, Lior Lev Sercarz, has been routinely identified as a "promotional book".

This book is called Art of Blending yet nowhere in the book is there anything about blending spices, recipes for spice mixes, or even suggestions how one might mix spices. Instead it seems like a book to sell spices from the authors store.

Therefore, my mission has become to personally explore and discover tea blending the the hope to eventually publish my findings and consequently provide a means for others to begin blending
